Hospitality Management Course

SBIHM offers one of the most renowned hospitality management courses in Kolkata. The graduate degree program in Hotel and Hospitality Management provides all students with an academic qualification tailored to the needs of experienced or aspiring managers who wish to expand their knowledge, skills and equip themselves for senior management positions.

The training in soft skills has two parts. One part involves developing attitudes and attributes, and the other part involves fine-tuning communication skills to express attitudes, ideas, and thoughts well.

Upon graduating from this hospitality management college in Kolkata; graduates shall become accomplished managers. They would be capable of working across a range of public and private enterprises including leisure facilities, hotels, theme parks, conferences, exhibitions and event management and etc.

Diploma in Hospitality Management

This two-year diploma program is designed for those who have completed their secondary education and are looking for a career in the hospitality industry or the international business sector. This hospitality management institute in Kolkata has the most pragmatic program.

The diploma course has a dual focus on developing a broad understanding of the operational aspects of the international hospitality industry and building knowledge of key business and management principles.

Duration:

  • • It comprises of four semesters including 6 – 12 months paid internship in India or overseas.
  • • Successfully completed higher secondary education (or equivalent).

Eligibility to join Diploma Training in Hospitality Management:

• Minimum 16 years of age with successfully completed secondary education

Bachelor in Hospitality Management

The BA degree for hospitality management training at SBIHM has been crafted and curated by industry experts. These industry professionals have jotted down a curriculum which is best suited to equip aspiring hospitality managers to be efficient and suited to perform their best in hospitality jobs.

At SBIHM the hospitality management training is conducted in a very unique, educational, and interactive manner, which gives ample scope for the students to interact with each other and face a wide variety of issues, topics, and situations that they are likely to come across as hospitality managers

Eligibility to join the Graduate Degree Program for Hospitality Management:

• Minimum 18 years of age with a successfully completed Diploma (or equivalent)

• Successfully completed higher secondary education (or equivalent).

How SBIHM will prepare you with the skills and confidence to launch a great career

Our dedicated career counselors play a valuable role in helping students achieve career success. The Careers Development and Industry Placement teams will help you to find an internship and first job that matches your skills and development objectives. They offer comprehensive support for:

  • The application process, including introduction to hiring practices.
  • CV preparation & Interview skills.
  • Internship and employment research methods
  • For the internship application process, they will also help narrow your focus, factoring in your language and location preferences and visa/work permit restrictions. Their support continues throughout the internship, ensuring you make the most out of your work experience.
  • On campus and off campuses are regularly held at SBIHM, where leading hospitality companies come to recruit talent.

Professional Internships

Fixed-term work placements in the hotel and hospitality sector form a key part of your diploma. They offer you hands-on experience and a chance to put into practice what you have learned on campus. Your internship experience will enable you to develop confidence, teamwork, leadership skills and a professional attitude, and help you secure your first step on the career ladder.

The Careers Development and Industry Placement teams of SBIHM will assist you in finding an internship that matches your skills and development objectives.

Faculty

Our faculty at SBIHM have excellent academic credentials and extensive experience in the industry. They are also talented educators, mentors and guides, dedicated to supporting students in achieving academic and career success.
