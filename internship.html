Hospitality

The Benefits of Hospitality Internship Programmes Hospitality Internship are a great way to learn and gain practical work experience for anyone who wants to pursue a career in the Hospitality sector. Career Training Internships builds partnerships with hotels and restaurants to provide hospitality training internships to students from across the world who are interested in building their careers in the hospitality sector.

Internship Opportunities - Hotel / Hospitality

Hospitality interns have the opportunity to travel and explore allover the world as thehospitality industry seeks diversity in its employees and looks for people from different backgrounds and cultures. Hospitality internships often are the first opportunity for students to gain work experience abroad; fortunately, for those seeking a career in the industry, there will be endless opportunities to travel and live abroad while further developing career.

Most common internship positions in the hotel / Hospitality management industry:

  • Operations trainee
  • Hotel management trainee
  • Management trainee
  • Marketing trainee
  • Front desk receptionist trainee
  • Human resources management trainee
  • Admin executive trainee
  • F&B trainee

Hospital

Securing a top-notch internship in Hospital Management is a strategic step towards a rewarding and successful career in the Hospital Industry. A highly respected internship is a great way to gain direct experiential learning that will help to find a position in the field. Many of these internship opportunities are provided by hospitals, and healthcare organizations.Internships provide students with valuable hands-on training, mentoring, and real-world experience. Internship is an ideal way to begin a professional career in the Hospital Management industry.

Fashion

Internships are an essential stage of learning for any career in any industry. Fashion design internships are very much important to understand the actual work of fashion industry. They can provide an amateur the exposure of the practical procedures of the fashion design job/career. Studying something in theory is very different from actually doing it and internships provide an opportunity to every student to implement their learning. Internships in the fashion industry enhance skills like creativity, research, analysis, sketching, using software and other tools etc. Thus, every student must go through this stage of internship before actually climbing the ladder of their career. Internships also give some clarity about your area of interest and acumen. Unless you try different job profiles it is difficult to take the decision of which exact branch of fashion you want to pick from the vast variety of options such as fashion styling, fashion designing, fashionmerchandising, blogging, etc.

While exploring new culinary techniques and practicing your skills in a school kitchen may make you a bettercook, there’s no replacing working in a commercial kitchen. That’s why culinary internships and externships are such an important part of culinary school. Internship is typeof experiential learning opportunities. Internships typically last longer. These are more general positions that can allow students to practice important culinary skills and get comfortable in a professional kitchen

Tourism

Be a part of the most exciting & growing Industry; Travel & Tourism. While working on the post as an Intern, learn about different interests, places & people. Day-to-day operations will be a part of the job, alongside marketing, internet & bookings will be an added learning advantage. Working on Itineraries & creating new programs for worldwide travellers would be there to learn. Interns can also get a chance to develop and add new destinations with the proper planning and discussion with the entire team. Get to learn about the start to the end of any travel program with the costing part that’s most important in the industry to learn. Come and explore the opportunity.

Job Description:
  • Arranging flights, insurance, and accommodation
  • Using a booking system to secure holidays
  • Collecting and processing payments
  • Advising clients on travel arrangements, e.g. visas and passports
  • Sending out tickets to clients
  • Dealing with complaints or refunds (not one of the perks, but someone's got to do it)
  • Marketing of the Travel programs
  • Creating Itineraries for festive travels
  • Accessing the scope of Travel in India
  • Social media promotion
  • Review of the current travel programs
  • Identifying the needs of a domestic or international traveller
  • Liaisoning with the local vendors like
  • Hotels
  • Taxi operators
  • Ticket Agents
  • Outbound tour operators
  • Planning to boost sales on special travel products & Others as per the needs
