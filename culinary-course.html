Culinary Management Course

Subhash Bose Institute of Hotel Management (SBIHM) is the top culinary institute in Kolkata. The three year Bachelor’s Degree in Culinary Arts, builds on the students’ culinary competencies and experiences to deepen and hone practical, leadership and business skills.

This culinary course in Kolkata starts with basic operational techniques and hands-on core competency training. Some core concepts which students shall be learning in their first year includes: classic cooking methods, cuts, stocks and sauces as a foundation stone. Students also learn about new product development, molecular cuisine, technology applications and managing teams in the kitchen labs provide additional capstone learning in a variety of skill areas before they start progressing to their final industry training and job placement.

Additionally, patisserie skills are developed through two specialized courses. At SBIHM, industry essentials of food safety and nutrition are taught to prepare students for their first industry training. Highlights of this three year degree program include inter-disciplinary management of food service operations, including customer service, resource planning and management.

Diploma in Culinary Arts

The SBIHM offers a diploma course in Culinary Arts. It introduces students to the world of food production through excellent career oriented training. Students learn all about the highlighted concepts of food, nutrition and mastering culinary skills. This diploma training is ranked as the best among all culinary colleges in Kolkata. Students get to advance and hone the potential of this culinary industry to the maximum capacity with this training program. Students are ensured high end placements and internships as beneficiaries of joining this culinary course.

Eligibility for joining Diploma in Culinary Arts at SBIHM:

  • • Minimum 16 years of age with successfully completed secondary education

Bachelor Degree in Culinary Arts

The three year Bachelor’s Degree in Culinary Arts, builds on the students’ culinary competencies and experiences to deepen and hone practical, leadership and business skills. It starts with basic operational techniques and hands-on core competency training. Highlights of this first year include classic cooking methods, cuts, stocks and sauces as a foundation stone. Patisserie skills are developed through two specialised courses and industry essentials of food safety and nutrition are taught in preparation for the students’ first industry training. Highlights of this three year program include inter-disciplinary management of food service operations, including customer service, resource planning and management. New product development, molecular cuisine, technology applications and managing teams in the kitchen labs provide additional capstone learning in a variety of skill areas before students progress to their final industry training and job placement.

Eligibility for joining Diploma in Culinary Arts at SBIHM:

  • • Minimum 18 years of age with a successfully completed Diploma (or equivalent)
  • • Successfully completed higher secondary education (or equivalent).

How SBIHM will prepare you with the skills and confidence to launch a great career

Our dedicated career counselors play a valuable role in helping students achieve career success. The Careers Development and Industry Placement teams will help you to find an internship and first job that matches your skills and development objectives. They offer comprehensive support for:

  • The application process, including introduction to hiring practices.
  • CV preparation & Interview skills.
  • Internship and employment research methods
  • For the internship application process, they will also help narrow your focus, factoring in your language and location preferences and visa/work permit restrictions. Their support continues throughout the internship, ensuring you make the most out of your work experience.
  • On campus and off campuses are regularly held at SBIHM, where leading hospitality companies come to recruit talent.

Professional Internships

Fixed-term work placements in the hotel and hospitality sector form a key part of your diploma. They offer you hands-on experience and a chance to put into practice what you have learned on campus. Your internship experience will enable you to develop confidence, teamwork, leadership skills and a professional attitude, and help you secure your first step on the career ladder.

The Careers Development and Industry Placement teams of SBIHM will assist you in finding an internship that matches your skills and development objectives.
