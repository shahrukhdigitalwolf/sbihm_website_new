Bachelor In Business Administration

BBA graduates can look for jobs in admin, finance, marketing department of various companies as members of management teams or as management trainees.After completing a BBA, students should consider broadening their horizon in order to qualify for top executive positions and promotions. Since the degree will equip a student of BBA with the basic knowledge about corporate management and skills it will help them understand the diverse functioning of a company.

Thus, a student of BBA could hit the glass ceiling very fast. Master and Doctoral programs are also available in business administration, which can prepare business professionals for work as consultants, executives or university professors.
