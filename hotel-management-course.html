Hotel Management Course

Subhash Bose IHM offers one of the best hotel management courses in Kolkata. Both the Bachelor and Master Degree programs in Hotel Management are offered by the National Council for Hotel Management and Catering Technology. Being affiliated to such a prestigious university, this degree holds much more value than any other typical hotel management course.

Subhash Bose IHM has been ranked as one of the best hotel management colleges in Kolkata. This three year degree program equips trainees with the required skills, knowledge and attitude to efficiently discharge supervisory responsibilities in the Hotel Airline and Cruise Line sector. The Bachelor of Science program comprises in-depth laboratory work for students.

In this way they are prepared to adapt the required knowledge and skill standards in several operational areas. The areas of expertise which are taught at Subhash Bose IHM are Food Production, Food and Beverage Service, Front Office Operation and House Keeping. This course also imparts substantial managerial inputs in subject such as Sales and Marketing, Financial Management, Human Resource Management, Hotel and Catering Law, Property Management, Entrepreneurship Development and other related subjects. The hotel management course fee at Subhash Bose IHM is very affordable for students.

B.Sc in Hospitality & Hotel Administration
  • Course Duration: 3 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 passed/appeared from recognized Central or State Govt. Board
  • Seat Availability: 60
  • Industrial training duration: 6 months only in 5 star deluxe hotel
  • The JNU (Jawaharlal Nehru University) shall award Degree to those students of the Institute who successfully qualify in all the prescribed examinations conducted by the Council.

Food Production & Patisserie

There was a time when cooking was purely an art but now it is described as both an art and a technology. Our hotel management institute has developed the most advanced and technically apprehensions worthy course which will help students understand food production and patisserie. It is said that we eat with our eyes, nose, mouth (tongue) and stomach, and a chef has to not only make the food edible, but he has to take care of the eye-appeal and gastronomic values of each item. However, this is appropriately compensated with high salaries and an immensely creative work environment. Food production is essentially a skilled and creative work of practical nature. Good chefs throughout their careers can always command status, respect and a high salary. This department at our hotel management institute deals with the preparation, production and presentation of foods from different cuisines from all over the world. Knowledge of the multitude of ingredients used to prepare items from different cuisines, their nutritional values, effects of different cooking methods of each ingredient – all are discussed in details in the hotel management classes. Each student is given ample opportunity to apply the knowledge and transfer it to multi-dimensional skill in preparing and presenting a large cross-section of Indian and internal cuisines in the practical sessions. Layout of kitchens and the allied sections, the vast range of tools and equipment, costing and budgeting of the food and overheads, etc. are also taught in details with the students handling real-life case studies. Creativity takes a front seat in the working of this department. Students who aspire to develop as chefs are encouraged to display their skills in ice-carving, butter-sculpture, sugar craft; designer bread-making, etc. during the several Food Festivals and functions (theme lunch/dinner, etc.) held every year. The Institute has four large kitchens equipped with all that match the standards of the industry. One ultramodern bakery and one self-dependent Confectionary are open to the students to hone their skills in the science and art of patisserie.
