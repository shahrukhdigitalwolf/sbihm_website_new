About Subhash Bose IHM

A Few Words About the Subhash Bose IHM

In Kolkata, where culture meets innovation, there’s a Central Government & State Government accredited institution that doesn’t just teach hospitality and health care, but crafts legends. Welcome to Subhas Bose Institute of Hotel Management, the beacon of excellence in Hospitality and Health care management education. Where dreams take flight and passions turn into international professions. Where creativity meets precision. Where hospitality & Health Care is not just a career, but a way of life. SBIHM offering Bachelor, Master, Diploma course in Hotel & Hospitality Management, Hospital & Health Care Management, Fashion Technology.

At SBIHM, we don’t just offer education; we orchestrate experiences. Our passionate faculty, cutting-edge facilities, and industry-immersive programmes redefine the way you perceive hospitality. Here, we don’t follow trends; we set them.

We are the crème de la crème of the National & International as acknowledged by luminaries such Former President Sri Pranab Mukherjee, Chef Sanjeev Kapoor, deputy PM, Thailand, Kon Kun Dabbaransi, Princess Isabelle Lafforguem, France, Fromer Indian Capten Kapil Dev, Gavaskar, Sourav Ganguly, Author Sri Sirshendu Mukherjee, Srijat, Padmashree Chef Sanjiv Kapoor, Rabirbrar, Dr. Kunal Sarkar.

Whether you aspire to be a top chef, GM, Doctor, a skilled planner, or a savvy hotel and Hospital manager, SBIHM provides its wards with a comprehensive education that goes beyond textbooks. Our hands-on training programmes coupled with international internships in leading hotels, Airline, Cruiseline, and superspeciality Hospital give all a competitive edge in the job market. Our industry-relevant curriculum ensures that our students are equipped with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the dynamic world of hospitality and Health Care Industry.

But it’s not just about academic excellence at SBIHM; it’s also about fostering a culture of warmth and empathy. Ours is a vibrant community where ideas flourish and talents shine. It’s where mentors don’t just teach; they inspire. Our students don’t just graduate; they transform into industry leaders, armed with knowledge, empathy, and a passion for hospitality and Health care Sector.

Join us at SBIHM Kolkata, where dreams don’t just take flight; they soar. Your journey starts here, in an institution that believes in your potential, nurtures your talents, and propels you towards a future adorned with stars.

  • Enrol today and embark on a journey that will transform your passion into a profession. Visit our website or call us to schedule a campus tour. Your extraordinary career in hospitality begins here, at SBIHM Kolkata.

SBIHM under UGC 2(F), Minister of HRD, Govt. of India, SBIHM also affiliated National Council for Hotel Management & Catering Technology, Ministry of Tourism, Govt. of India, SBIHM Recognized by Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, Govt. of West Bengal.
  • It consciously creates an environment in classrooms to broaden intellectual horizon through better pedagogy, improved, infrastructure, integration of emerging educational technologies and expanded study abroad programme aiming at Global Education.
  • Ideal location : Subhash Bose IHM enjoys prime location in the most developing part of the city with an easy access to public transport, well developed road network, metro connectivity and at the time same time surrounded by greenery and natural ambience.
  • 100% Job assurance on Graduation day – since 1998 Subhash Bose IHM has consistently maintained a successful student employment track record on the convocation day, Over 93% of our alumni have found lucrative employment within a year of graduation. This unrivalled record is a reflection of competency, diligence, learning curve and achievements of students through out their curse at Subhash Bose IHM.
  • Unique learning curve – Subhash Bose IHM has implemented a Unique system of education by dividing a semester into four mini unit terms of 5 weeks each. This system enables students to focus only on 3 to 4 academic subjects at a time instead of the usual 8 – 10 subject as in other management schools. As a result, Subhash Bose IHM students are able to complete 12-14 academic subjects within a 6 month period without pressurizing or coercing them.
  • The institution offers various value added programmes to inculcate the ability to think critically and objectively. It understands while every student has learning barriers, he / she has amazing strengths. Subhash Bose IHM encourages those strengths and teach students to be proud themselves and what they can accomplish.
  • Last but not the least, Subhash Bose IHM offers scholarships and award as well as financial assistance to deserving students. To enhance multicultural and cosmopolitan nature it promotes diversity in all spheres

CAMPUS DETAILS

We provides the opportunity to prepare for life

The Subhash Bose IHM takes pride in its excellence in a resourceful library.

An educational oasis located at dream city of Ms. Mamata Banerjee, Chief minister, West Bengal with green lushes along its sides, The Subhash Bose IHM Campus is a goldmine of opportunity for students.

The Subhash Bose IHM houses the Boys’ and the Girls’ Hostels also outside of the main college campus.

The college has two separate common rooms one for the girls and the other for the boys. Recreational facilities are available in the common room.

It is 15 minutes away from Saltlake city, Sector – V, end no of buses, taxi, auto services are there. Bus stop ECO park Gate No. 1 or Subhas Hotel Management college.
