CEO Speak's

Subhash Bose IHM, Kolkata was started by Subhas Bose Institute of Hotel Management has grown strength to strength and today it is recognized across the globe for quality education for many years. When you join Subhash Bose IHM, you join a global community. We boast campuses located in stunning parts of Bengal. We blend a traditional model of hospitality education with hands-on learning, exciting internships, insightful industry visits and cutting-edge hospitality knowledge.

We at Subhash Bose IHM believe on socio-economic transformation through value based education. The institute offers disciplined environment facilitating the students to learn as a team, think out of the box and to be creative. Every student is nurtured here to bring out the best from him/her. Extra-curricular activities are also given due importance in the institute. We would like to ensure that every passing out student of Subhash Bose IHM would be a responsible professional as well as a thoughtful member of the society.

The faculty members are well qualified and highly motivated. They are alumni of premier institutions and possess rich experience of teaching, research and industry. I am sure that every student of Subhash Bose IHM will make a niche in the professional world. I wish all the students of Subhash Bose IHM, a very bright future.

Mrs. Bidisha Sarkar
CEO
Subhash Bose IHM
